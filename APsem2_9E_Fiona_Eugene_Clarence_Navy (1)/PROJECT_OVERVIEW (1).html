🎯 Project Overview

This is a comprehensive e-learning platform designed to teach AP Physics concepts through interactive multimedia content, original SVG diagrams, embedded educational videos, and interactive calculators. The website is fully responsive and includes 7 content pages plus supporting materials.

📚 Educational Content

🚀 Kinematics

Motion, velocity, acceleration, and projectile motion with interactive calculator and motion diagrams.

⚡ Forces & Motion

Newton's laws, free body diagrams, friction, tension, and circular motion analysis.

⚡ Energy & Work

Kinetic and potential energy, conservation of energy, power, with energy calculator.

〰️ Waves & Oscillation

Simple harmonic motion, wave properties, interference, standing waves, and sound.
